# 📚 VANGUARD: DEEP CURRICULUM (Source 02)

> **Document Type:** Detailed Module Breakdown
> **Context:** Includes "Golden Thread" and recent improvements.

---

## 🟢 MODULE 1: THE INTELLIGENCE INSIDE
**"The biology of the machine."**

### Core Concepts
*   **Neural Networks:** Visualized as biological layers, not just math.
*   **Tokenization:** The "atoms" of language. Teaches how computers read.
*   **Probability:** The "Next Token Prediction" engine.
*   **Ethics:** Bias, Hallucination, and the "Black Box" problem.

### The "Golden Thread" Project (Phase 1)
*   **Project:** *Design the Brain.*
*   **Task:** Define the personality, knowledge base, and ethical boundaries of a Personal AI Assistant.
*   **Outcome:** A text-based "System Prompt" that defines *who* the AI is.

---

## 🟠 MODULE 2: AGENTS & AUTOMATION
**"The hands of the machine."**

### Core Concepts
*   **Agency:** The ability to *act*, not just speak.
*   **Tools:** Connecting LLMs to the real world (Email, Calendar, Search).
*   **Chain of Thought:** How an agent "plans" a task step-by-step.
*   **Logic:** If/Then/Else structures for controlling robots.

### The "Golden Thread" Project (Phase 2)
*   **Project:** *Build the Body.*
*   **Task:** Connect the "Brain" from Mod 1 to real tools. Give it the ability to read email and schedule meetings.
*   **Outcome:** A functional "Agent" that can perform tasks autonomously.

---

## 🟣 MODULE 3: GENERATIVE INTELLIGENCE
**"The imagination of the machine."**

### Core Concepts
*   **Diffusion Models:** How images emerge from noise.
*   **Latent Space:** The mathematical "map" of all possible concepts.
*   **Multi-Modal:** Text-to-Image, Text-to-Video, Text-to-Audio.
*   **Deepfakes:** Detection, creation, and ethics of synthetic media.

### The "Golden Thread" Project (Phase 3)
*   **Project:** *Give it a Face.*
*   **Task:** Generate an avatar, voice, and promotional video for the Agent from Mod 2.
*   **Outcome:** A full media package for the AI Assistant.

---

## ⚪ MODULE 4: GOVERNANCE & PRODUCTION
**"The conscience of the machine."**

### Core Concepts
*   **Governance:** Rules, policies, and "Constitutions" for AI.
*   **Deployment:** Moving from "localhost" to the cloud.
*   **Security:** Protection against Prompt Injection and Jailbreaks.
*   **Enterprise Scale:** Managing fleets of agents.

### The "Golden Thread" Project (Phase 4)
*   **Project:** *Incorporate the Entity.*
*   **Task:** Write the "User Manual" and "Governance Policy" for the Assistant. Deploy it live.
*   **Outcome:** A fully governed, deployed, and safe AI product. (The "Capstone").
